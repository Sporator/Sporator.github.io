Cortez and the Montezuma County high desert see more sun and less snow than the mountains to the east, with their own set of challenges: relentless UV, wind, and the occasional hailstorm near Mesa Verde country.

Universal Roofing serves the Cortez area with roofing built for high-desert sun, plus full-service contracting through Universal Handyman.

High-desert sun and hail

Out west the enemy is less snow and more sun, wind, and hail. UV bakes cheaper roofing fast at this altitude, and a single hailstorm can do real damage. We spec materials that stand up to that, and we know what hail damage actually looks like on a roof.
