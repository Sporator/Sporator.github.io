Durango is the heart of La Plata County and where Universal Roofing does much of its work. Between historic homes downtown, newer builds up in the hills, and everything in between, Durango roofs see the full range of Southwest Colorado weather: intense high-altitude sun, real winter snow, and the late-summer monsoon.

We have installed, replaced, and repaired roofs across Durango since 2005, and through our Universal Handyman division we handle the rest of the project too, from framing to finish.

Durango's mix of old and new

Durango's housing runs from century-old homes near downtown to modern construction in the surrounding hills, and each needs a different approach. Older roofs often hide surprises in the decking; newer ones are about getting the details right for our altitude and snow. We will tell you honestly which yours needs.
