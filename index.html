About Universal Roofing

Universal Roofing has been serving La Plata County and surrounding areas with quality roofing services since 2005. As a licensed and insured company, we take pride in our craftsmanship and commitment to customer satisfaction.

We specialize in all types of roofing systems including metal, shingles, tile, shake, asphalt, and E.P.D.M. for both residential and commercial properties. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing the highest quality roofing solutions tailored to meet your specific needs.
