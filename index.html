About Universal Roofing

Universal Roofing is a family owned and operated business serving La Plata County and surrounding areas since 2005. Roofing is in our blood, second generation at it, and it is still the heart of what we do, on metal, shingles, tile, shake, asphalt, and E.P.D.M. for both residential and commercial properties.

For everything else around the property, our Universal Handyman division handles full-service and one-off jobs, from framing, drywall, and decks to remodels, siding, stucco, paint, and repairs. Licensed and insured, we take pride in our craftsmanship and stand behind our work.
