About Universal Roofing

Universal Roofing is a family owned and operated business serving La Plata County and surrounding areas since 2005. We started in roofing, second generation at it, and have grown into a full-service contractor that takes on the whole project.

Today we handle roofing, framing, new builds, remodels and additions, decks, siding, stucco, paint, and repairs. Backed by a trusted network of local trades, we can run an entire job under one contractor, so you are not juggling five different crews. Licensed and insured, we take pride in our craftsmanship and stand behind our work.
