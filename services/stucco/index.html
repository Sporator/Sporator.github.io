Stucco

Stucco application and repair done to last in our climate.

Stucco is common across the Southwest for good reason, but it has to be applied and repaired correctly or it cracks and lets water in. Universal Handyman handles stucco application and repair.

We address cracks and failures at the source so the repair holds.

What's included

  • Stucco application
  • Crack and damage repair
  • Color and texture matching
  • Patching and re-coating
  • Sealing against moisture

Cracks and freeze-thaw

Stucco cracks are not just cosmetic here. Once water gets behind stucco, freeze-thaw widens the crack and damages what is underneath. Catching and sealing cracks early is far cheaper than repairing the wall later.

Frequently asked questions

Can you match my existing stucco texture and color?

We work to match texture and color so repairs blend in, though exact matches on older or sun-faded stucco can be challenging and we will set honest expectations.

Why is my stucco cracking?

Some hairline cracking is normal as a building settles. Wider or spreading cracks can signal a moisture or structural issue worth looking at before it grows.
