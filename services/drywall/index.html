Drywall

Hang, tape, mud, and texture, finished smooth and ready for paint.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

Good drywall work disappears: flat walls, crisp corners, and texture that matches the rest of the house. Our Universal Handyman division hangs, finishes, and repairs drywall for remodels, additions, and repairs.

From a single patched hole to a whole new room, we leave it ready for paint.

What's included

  • Drywall hanging and installation
  • Taping, mudding, and sanding
  • Texture matching
  • Water and impact damage repair
  • Ceilings and patch work

Repairs that match

The hardest part of a drywall repair is making it invisible, which means matching the existing texture. That finish detail is exactly the kind of thing our crew sweats so a patch does not stand out.

Frequently asked questions

Can you match my existing wall texture?

Yes. Texture matching is a big part of repair work, and we take the time to blend a patch into the surrounding wall or ceiling.

Do you handle water-damaged drywall?

We do, including cutting out the damaged section, addressing the moisture source where we can, and finishing it back to match.
