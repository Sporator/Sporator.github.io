Painting

Interior and exterior painting with proper prep and durable finishes.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

A paint job is only as good as the prep underneath it. Universal Handyman handles interior and exterior painting, with the surface prep that makes a finish last.

We will help you choose products rated for our sun and seasons so the color holds.

What's included

  • Interior and exterior painting
  • Surface prep, patching, and priming
  • Trim, doors, and accents
  • UV-rated exterior coatings
  • Clean, careful work

Finishes built for the sun

Exterior paint at altitude fights intense UV that fades and chalks cheaper finishes fast. Proper prep and the right product are what stand between you and repainting every few years.

Frequently asked questions

Do you do interior and exterior?

Both. Exterior work is weather-dependent and best done in the warmer, drier months; interiors we can do year round.

How much prep is included?

Prep is the part that makes paint last, so we patch, sand, and prime as needed rather than painting over problems.
