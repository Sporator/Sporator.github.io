Foundation Coating & Waterproofing

Keep water out of your foundation and out of your home.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

Water is the slow enemy of any building. Universal Roofing applies foundation coatings and waterproofing to keep snowmelt and monsoon runoff from working its way into your foundation and basement.

If you are seeing damp walls, efflorescence, or seasonal seepage, we can assess the source and recommend the right protection.

What's included

  • Foundation coatings and sealing
  • Below-grade waterproofing
  • Moisture and drainage assessment
  • Crack sealing
  • Protection against snowmelt and monsoon runoff

Snowmelt and monsoon season

In La Plata County the two big water events are spring snowmelt and the late-summer monsoon, when heavy rain hits dry ground fast. Both push water against foundations. Coating and proper drainage are far cheaper than repairing water damage after it gets inside.

Frequently asked questions

How do I know if my foundation needs waterproofing?

Damp or stained basement walls, a white powdery residue (efflorescence), musty smells, or seasonal seepage are all signs water is getting in. We can take a look and tell you.

When is the best time to do this work?

Dry conditions are ideal for coatings to cure properly, so late spring through early fall usually works best, ahead of the next snow season.
