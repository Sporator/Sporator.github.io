Commercial Roofing

Flat, low-slope, and metal roofing for businesses and commercial properties.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

Commercial roofs have different demands than homes: larger spans, flat and low-slope systems, rooftop equipment, and the need to keep a business running while the work gets done. Universal Roofing installs, recoats, and repairs commercial roofing throughout La Plata County.

We will assess the existing system, lay out your options clearly, and schedule the work to keep disruption to your operation as low as possible.

What's included

  • Flat and low-slope roof systems
  • EPDM and single-ply membranes
  • Metal commercial roofing
  • Roof coatings and restoration
  • Repairs and maintenance programs

Less downtime, fewer surprises

Flat and low-slope roofs are where our climate finds weaknesses fastest, especially where snow sits and meltwater pools. Regular inspection and coating can add years to a commercial roof before a full replacement is ever needed, and we will tell you honestly which stage yours is at.

Frequently asked questions

What flat roof systems do you install?

We work with EPDM and single-ply membranes, metal, and protective coating systems. The right choice depends on your building, slope, and budget.

Can you work around our business hours?

Yes. We schedule commercial work to minimize disruption and will coordinate around your operation wherever possible.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

We can set up periodic inspection and maintenance so small issues get caught before they become expensive leaks.
