New Builds

Whole-project new construction, coordinated through one accountable contractor.

A new build pulls together every trade, and the value of one accountable contractor is that you are not the one chasing five different crews. Through Universal Handyman, we take on new construction backed by a trusted network of local trades.

From framing through finish, we keep the project moving and keep you informed.

What's included

  • Whole-project new construction
  • Framing through finish work
  • Coordination of trusted local trades
  • One point of accountability
  • Built to local code and climate

One contractor, whole job

On a new build the gaps between trades are where problems hide. Running the project through one contractor with a known set of subs closes those gaps, which is the same whole-job thinking behind everything Universal Roofing does.

Frequently asked questions

Do you build whole homes or just parts?

We take on whole-project new construction and coordinate the trades, and we also do individual scopes like framing or decks if that is all you need.

How does the subcontractor network work?

We bring in trusted local trades we have worked with, and we stay the single point of accountability so you are dealing with one contractor, not many.
