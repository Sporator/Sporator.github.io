Decks

Decks built to handle mountain weather and last.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

A deck is where you actually use Colorado, so it should be solid underfoot and built to survive our winters. Universal Handyman designs and builds decks, from simple platforms to multi-level outdoor space.

We build with materials and footings that hold up to freeze-thaw and snow load.

What's included

  • New deck design and construction
  • Wood and composite decking
  • Railings and stairs
  • Proper footings for freeze-thaw
  • Repairs and rebuilds

Footings that survive winter

Frost heave is real here. Footings that are not set below the frost line will lift and rack a deck over a few winters. We build the part you cannot see correctly so the part you enjoy stays level and safe.

Frequently asked questions

Wood or composite?

Wood costs less up front and looks classic; composite costs more but resists our intense UV and needs less upkeep. We will help you weigh it for your spot and budget.

Can you repair or rebuild an existing deck?

Yes. We can replace decking and railings, reinforce framing, or rebuild from the footings up if the structure is unsafe.
