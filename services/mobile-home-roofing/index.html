Mobile Home Roofing

Roof-overs, coatings, and leak repair built for manufactured homes.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

Mobile and manufactured homes have roofing needs all their own, and many roofers will not take them on. Universal Roofing does. We install metal roof-overs, apply coatings, and fix leaks on manufactured homes across the area.

A proper roof-over can stop chronic leaks, add insulation, and extend the life of the home for a fraction of the cost of constant patching.

What's included

  • Metal roof-overs
  • Roof coatings and sealing
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Added insulation under the new roof
  • Inspections and maintenance

Why a roof-over

Flat and low-slope mobile home roofs tend to pond water and leak at the seams, and our freeze-thaw cycles make it worse every winter. A sloped metal roof-over sheds snow and water, cuts heating loss, and usually costs far less than ongoing repairs.

Frequently asked questions

Roof-over or full replacement?

For most manufactured homes a metal roof-over is the better value: it solves the leaks, adds insulation, and lasts. We will inspect yours and tell you what makes sense.

Will a roof-over help with heating?

Usually yes. We can add insulation under the new roof, which often makes a noticeable difference in comfort and heating cost.
