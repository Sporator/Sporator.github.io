Siding

Siding that protects your home and stands up to high-altitude sun.

Call (970) 759-6008 for a Free Estimate

Siding is your home's first layer of weather protection and a big part of how it looks. Universal Handyman installs and repairs siding to keep your walls sealed against wind, snow, and sun.

We will help you pick a material that handles our climate and matches the look you want.

What's included

  • New siding installation
  • Siding repair and replacement
  • Trim and flashing
  • Weather sealing
  • Material and color guidance

Sun and weather sealing

High-altitude UV fades and degrades siding faster than it does lower down, and wind-driven snow finds any gap in the flashing. Correct installation and sealing are what keep siding doing its job through our seasons.

Frequently asked questions

Do you repair just a damaged section?

Yes. If only part of the siding is damaged we can replace that section and blend it, or we can re-side the whole home if it is time.

How is this related to roofing?

Siding and roofing meet at flashing details that have to be watertight. As roofers, getting those transitions right is second nature to us.
